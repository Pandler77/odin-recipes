PIZZA

This is pepporoni pizza, the greatest of all pizzas. With equisite dough, a sweet yet savory sauce, loads of mozzarella cheese and to top it all off, gorgeous crusted pepporonis.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough (flour, yeast, water, sugar, salt, oilive oil)
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Low-Moisture Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni Slices

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Add Sauce
  3. Add Cheese
  4. Add Toppings
  5. Cook
