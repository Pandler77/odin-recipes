Steak

A juicy, tender, basted, delicious steak.

Ingredients

  • Steak (whichever cut is preferred)
  • Garlic cloves
  • Rosemary
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Place steak into hot skillet.
  2. Cook until internal temp reaches 130°F to 135°F, or your preferred doneness.
  3. Add butter, garlic cloves, and rosemary to the pan.
  4. Baste steak 10 to 20 times.
  5. Remove from pan and let steak rest.
