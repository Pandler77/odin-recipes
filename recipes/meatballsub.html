Meatball Sub

A delicious tomato based sandwich with juicy ground meat rolled into balls placed onto a piece of french bread

Ingredients

  • Meatballs
  • Mozzarella
  • Tomato Sauce
  • French Bread

Steps

  1. Put tomato sauce in pan on medium heat
  2. Wait for tomato sauce to boil
  3. Place meatballs into sauce to cook
  4. Cut bread in half and put in oven to toast
  5. When meatballs are to temperature, place tomato sauce then meatballs on bread
  6. Sprinkle Mozzarella on top of meatballs
  7. Place sub into broiler for 2 minutes to let cheese melt
